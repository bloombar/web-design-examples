Koala fact sheet

Koalas originate from the hinterlands of the far west of Australia. They are fuzzy, but surprisingly like to get drunk on fermented sap of the trees in which they reside.

  • Longevity: 13-18 years
  • Males may die sooner because of their more hazardous lives
  • Baby koalas are known as Joeys
  • Sleep 20 hours a day
  • Two opposable thumbs on each hand!
